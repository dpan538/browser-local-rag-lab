# Reports

This folder is the research report home for the standalone
`browser-local-rag-lab` repository. Reports placed here belong to the research
repo, not to the archive product process.

## Deep Research Reports

- `Browser-Local Qwen RAG Optimization for a Rights-Aware Graphic Design Archive.docx`
  - Original archive-origin optimization report.
- `Browser-Local Small-Model RAG for a Rights-Aware Archive.docx`
  - Archive-origin framing for evidence packets, answer lanes, faithfulness,
    and rights-aware RAG.
- `WebGPU Runtime Study for Browser-Local Small-Model RAG.docx`
  - Runtime-focused framing for WebGPU, WebLLM, Transformers.js, ONNX Runtime
    WebGPU, cache behavior, TTFT, and reproducible latency measurement.
